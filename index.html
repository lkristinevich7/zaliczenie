Services

Landing Page/Promo website

Landing Page is the target web page, which main goal is to force the visitor to perform a specific action, which is targeted, and transfer him from ordinary site visitor to the category of buyers of those goods or services that are advertised on this page.

Portfolio site

Portfolio site - is first of all an online exhibition of any own work performed. It can be useful to a designer, artist, photographer - to any author who wants to express himself, as well as trying to find customers, buyers for his works.

eCommerce website

Trade in goods via the Internet is the most promising and successfully developing business line. An online store can be a great addition to an offline business, as well as a major source of income.

Business card website

A business card site is the simplest and most common web resource that serves to familiarize oneself with a company, its products and services. This site answers many questions and is one of the sources for attracting customers.

Corporate/business website

A corporate website is a high-quality and complete presentation of a company on the Internet. Its main goal is to attract new customers or partners, to develop a business by disclosing the advantages of the company, a detailed description of its activities, services or products.

Personal blog

Personal blog is a web site the main content of which is regularly added posts (posts) containing text, images, multimedia. Using a blog, you can create a vision of your organization, influence the impressions of products and services, thereby promoting them.
